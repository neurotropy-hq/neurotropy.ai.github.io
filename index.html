Our Vision

The brain is the missing variable in learning

We've all sat down to study and stared at the page. Not because the material was too hard, but because our brain simply wasn't ready yet. That can be pretty frustrating. So we started asking why.

It turns out your capacity to learn depends on the state of your brain. Some brain states support learning, others don't. And with the right feedback, you can learn to shift into the right one. Neurotropy gets you there.
